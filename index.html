About Me

I am an established leader seasoned by my years in hospitality and manufacturing, prepared to transition into a role as a full stack web developer. From childhood, I discovered a passion for tinkering with electronics and designing implementations for solutions to problems I was intrigued by. My love for technology was in full swing by the time I was building my first computer at age ten and watching it post. From that moment, I have been feeding an ever-growing appetite for technical knowledge which has led me to ongoing studies in web development. My recent studies at University of North Carolina Charlotte has allowed me to merge my love for people with full-stack development. I am particularly excited about serving businesses and organizations that make a positive difference in the lives of their people and surrounding communities. I want to hear from you, reach out and let me know how I can serve you and your business!
